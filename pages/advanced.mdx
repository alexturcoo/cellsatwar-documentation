# The Games

Cells at War has developed into a suite of STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, Mathematics) 
related video games that have the ability to be implemented in classrooms at post-secondary institutions.

Currently, games are available for topics in physics, biology, and music. 

## The Expert Deck

The expert deck is a unique feature built in to all games part of the Cells at War suite.
It provides an opportunity for students to interact with the material learned
in game in unique and engaging ways. For each game, the expert deck is done slighlty different however
all the expert decks provide accurate and detailed descriptions of important concepts from the games.
